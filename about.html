About

Liquid Cities is a project about building identity — the moments people map onto the buildings of a city.

Every day we move through cities that seem fixed — concrete, glass, stone. But a city is also liquid: it is remade by the feelings, memories and moments each of us pours into it. Liquid Cities asks a simple question — where are you from? — and lets people answer not with a place name, but with a moment.

How it works

In the app, you search for a building, confirm its real 3D mass, and map a photo or a short video of a moment onto it. Each mapped moment becomes a node on a shared map — a board, a flat map, or a rotating globe — where your city meets everyone else's.
